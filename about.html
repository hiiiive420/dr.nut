History of Dr. Nut

He used to be an animal surgeon and mostly treated eagles. He got an acorn stuck in them. Dr thought of a brilliant idea of making these nuts into drinks no one will get them stuck again. Looking at a dying legend, our team decided to bring back the brand Dr. Nut back to life...
